First-Semester Experience Survey

We invite undergraduate students from all universities in the world to evaluate the semester courses, corresponding to the first semester of 2020. The research aims to assess how the disciplines acted in the development of the necessary competences for the professional and citizen education of the students. For this, students can indicate their opinions according to their experiences during the semester with objective and essay questions from the research instrument. The student's identity will help us in the evaluation if we need more detailed information, depending on the consolidation of the answers, you authorize us to contact you for further clarification. We appreciate your participation!
